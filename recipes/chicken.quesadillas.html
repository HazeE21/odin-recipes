Chicken Quesadillas

Description

A chicken quesadilla is a Mexican-inspired dish made by filling a flour tortilla with seasoned chicken, cheese, and sometimes other ingredients, then grilling or pan-frying it until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese is melted and gooey

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 lb chicken breast cut into small pieces
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2 large bell pepper diced
  • 1/2 yellow onion diced
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Flour tortillas
  • 2 cups mexican cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet with oil on medium heat
  2. Add in the chicken and taco seasoning. Cook for 4 mins
  3. Add in the bell pepper and onion. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, then set to side
  4. On skillet, add butter or oil and place a tortilla on top
  5. Add mexican cheese and chicken mixture to half of the tortilla. Top off with more cheese
  6. Fold the tortilla over to close it
  7. Cook for a few minutes until the tortilla is golden brown. Flip over and repeat
  8. Serve with some guacamole and sour cream
