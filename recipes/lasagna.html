Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the name of one of the oldest and best-known pasta shapes. It is usually rectangular or ribbon shaped, thicker than tagliatelle, made from a dough based on flour and eggs, with numerous local variants

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Ricotta mix
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Add meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles
  3. Spread ricotta mix on top
  4. Add another layer of meat sauce
  5. Repeat the same steps two more times
  6. Top off with Mozzarella cheese
  7. Cover and bake at 375 degress for 30 minutes
  8. Remove cover and bake for an additional 15 minutes
  9. Let rest for 15 minutes and serve
